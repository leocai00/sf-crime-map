Topic Description

I am interested in the topic of Safety, especially student safety. Now a day, crime and violence have become a huge problem. A lot of incidents happened around the campus. The crime rate has increased by more than 20% since the past few years in the University District. Last year, someone broke my car window and thieved my camera from my car. From then on, I never left any valuable things in a vehicle. I have also heard a lot of criminal incidents from my friends and received many UW Alter notifications. How shall we prevent crime around campus and how to ensure the safety of students are always the questions in my mind.

The school provides a lot of services like night ride and night walk to ensure studentsâ€™ safety and prevent crime, but still, there are plenty of students who do not use any of those services because it may take too long to wait for night ride or they are just lazy to request a night walk. Furthermore, the problem is not only for students but also for everyone. For example, A tourist who visited Los Angeles was attacked and got injured because he was not familiar with the city and accidental walk into a high crime area. I would like to raise a question of how to help a tourist to plan his/her routes in an unfamiliar city and avoid involving into criminal incidents? Let me introduce a few potential solutions for that issue in the following section.

Potential Web Applications

  • Safe Route Planer
  • Real Time Location Share and Emergency Alter

Safe Route Planer: The purpose of this app is to help people plan a safe walking route. The app will evaluate the safety of all the possible routes and recommend the safest one for the selected range of time. Most walking routes planning apps like Google Map only evaluate time and distance. The app can fill in the blank area and prevent people from crime.

Real Time Location Share and Emergency Alter: The purpose of this app is to help people share their location, when dangerous things happen. Users can notify the local police with their location and emergency, so they can get help immediately.
